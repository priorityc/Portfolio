British Meteorite Society

British meteor society is educational website with a potential to use Softwere that group reports of fireballs into Events. If more than three events are occured at same area they are grouped as an event. The number of events are displayed in a table sorted by area. This site also share information about different types of meteorites, so the users are easy to identify them. (BMS) is builded to track meteorites observation across England and might be used from proffesionals as well as amatiors.
