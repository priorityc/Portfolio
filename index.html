About Me

A few years ago, I made the decision to change my career and step into the world of technology. What started as a simple desire to build my own blog quickly grew into a passion for solving problems and understanding how digital products are made.

My journey began with an introductory web development course through the Raspberry Pi Foundation, and that experience sparked a deep curiosity to learn more.

Motivated by the need to pursue something meaningful, I enrolled in the Purple Beard Bootcamp — a decision that opened the door to an entirely new path.

Today, as I approach the final stage of my BSc in Computing & IT at The Open University, I can say with confidence that it has been one of the most rewarding choices of my life.git
